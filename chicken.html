Chicken Karai

Prep time: 10 mins

Servings: 2

Indian cuisine at its finest

Chicken Karahi is a traditional and immensely popular North Indian and Pakistani curry dish. It is named after the cooking vessel, the Karahi, a thick, circular, and deep cooking pot similar to a wok. This dish is characterized by its rich and aromatic gravy made from a blend of tomatoes, ginger, garlic, and a medley of spices, simmered to perfection with tender chicken pieces. The result is a tantalizingly spicy and flavorful curry that pairs wonderfully with naan or steamed rice.
